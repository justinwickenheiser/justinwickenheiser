Brotherhood

In the Fall of 2015, I decided to rush a fraternity. On November 20, 2015, I became a brother of the Delta Phi chaper of Alpha Sigma Phi Fraternity. This was one of the best decisions I ever made. I joined an amazing group of men, who I am proud to call my brothers. We work hard to raise money for our philanthropy, Canine Companions for Independence (CCI). You can learn more about CCI and everything they do at CCI.org.

Our biggest way to raise money for CCI is our annual BikeTrip. BikeTrip is an 8 day trip where the brothers who have raised a certain amount of money for CCI bike the perimeter of Lake Michigan. You can learn more about our BikeTrip by visiting the official Delta Phi Chapter's Philianthropy page.
