Marching Band

I have become heavily invested in the Laker Marching Band (LMB). I marched clarinet all four years of my undergrad. After my sophomore year, I became the uniform chairperson for the Laker Marching Band Council. I was in charge of all the marching band uniforms for three years, which is perhaps the longest any one person has done it.

Even after completing my undergrad, I have been helping the band in any way I can. I have continued managing the band's website, and I have either created or help facilitate the creation of different promotion materials. Each summer, I volunteer my time by organizing their band camp check-in process and by helping out throughout their band camp wherever I am needed.

Since I enjoy doing video editing, I try and get footage of the band that I can use to create a video header for their website. In the future, I hope to get aerial footage to use for another video header or for other promotional materials.
