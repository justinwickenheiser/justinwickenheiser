Brotherhood

In the fall of 2015, I decided to rush a fraternity. On November 20, 2015, I became a brother of the Delta Phi chaper of Alpha Sigma Phi fraternity. This was one of the best decisions I ever made. I joined an amazing group of men, who I am proud to call my brothers. We are working hard to raise money for our philanthropy, Canine Companions for Independence (CCI). You can learn more about CCI and everything they do at CCI.org.

Our biggest way to raise money for CCI is our annual BikeTrip. BikeTrip is an 8 day trip where the brothers who have raised at least a certain amount of money for CCI bike the perimeter of Lake Michigan. Churches and schools graciously donate their facilities for us to rest overnight in along the way. When we make it to Chicago, we deliver the check of everything we raised to CCI. If you would like to make a donation to CCI and the puppies, you can do so here.

Check out the video of last year's BikeTrip.
